About This Space

This is a space for sharing thoughts grounded in unbiased clinical research and analysis. Every article provides its evidence sources. When contradictory evidence exists, it gets published alongside the original analysis — because intellectual honesty matters more than being right.

The goal is straightforward: help people make more informed decisions about their mental health based on what the evidence actually shows — not what's being marketed to them.

— AJ Anderson, APRN, PMHNP-BC, LCSW

Clinical Supervision

Waypoint Cooperative provides clinical supervision to support your professional development, licensure requirements, and clinical excellence. We offer individual and group supervision in multiple modalities, led by experienced licensed supervisors.

Individual Supervision

One-on-one sessions tailored to your specific clinical needs and learning goals

Group Supervision

Collaborative peer learning and case consultation with fellow members

Triadic Supervision

Three-person format combining individual and peer consultation

Supervision by Credential Type

Select the supervision type that matches your licensure requirements:

LCSW Supervision

For Master's level clinical social workers

LMFT Supervision

For licensed marriage and family therapists

LADC Supervision

For licensed alcohol and drug counselors

Why Waypoint

At Waypoint Cooperative, we bring a unique approach to mental health care. As a worker-owned cooperative, our clinicians are invested in both your care and each other's success. We believe that multidisciplinary collaboration leads to better outcomes.

🤝

Collaborative Care Model

Your providers work together in a truly integrated way, sharing clinical insights and creating comprehensive treatment plans tailored to your unique needs.

👥

Multidisciplinary Team

Our clinicians include licensed therapists, psychiatric nurse practitioners, and addiction specialists—all bringing their expertise to your care.

💡

Evidence-Based Treatment

We combine the latest clinical research with compassionate care. CBT, DBT, EMDR, trauma-focused therapy, and medication management informed by evidence.

🌱

Worker-Owned & Community-Minded

Profits are reinvested in our team and community. Your care supports local mental health professionals committed to ongoing learning and social responsibility.

Insurance & Payment

We work with most major insurance plans. If you don't have insurance, we offer self-pay and sliding scale options.

Including Medicare, Medicaid, Anthem, Cigna, United Healthcare, Aetna, and many others. Contact us to verify coverage for your specific plan.
